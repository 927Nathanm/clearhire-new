FAQ

Common questions

How does the 7-day re-audit work?
After purchasing the Basic plan, you can upload and audit your resume as many times as you want for 7 days. Fix what it tells you, re-upload the updated version, and see your score improve. No limit on how many times you audit.
What does "lifetime" mean for the Pro plan?
Pay once and you can audit your resume unlimited times forever — across multiple job searches, career changes, or promotions. You also get unlimited AI rewrites on every audit.
Is the feedback specific to my resume?
Yes. The analysis reads your actual content and references specific sections, wording, and gaps. You won't receive generic tips — you'll get feedback like "your bullet points under Company X describe tasks, not outcomes."
What file formats are accepted?
PDF and plain text (.txt). If your PDF isn't reading correctly, open it in Word or Google Docs and export as plain text, then upload that file.
Is my resume stored or shared?
No. Your resume is processed in real time and not stored on our servers.
