Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use ClearHire, we collect the following information:

Payment information: Processed entirely by Stripe. We never see or store your credit card details.

Email address: Collected by Stripe during checkout for payment receipts. We do not use this for marketing without your consent.

Resume content: Uploaded temporarily for analysis only. Your resume is processed in real time and is not stored on our servers after analysis is complete.

Usage data: Standard server logs including IP address and browser type, used for security and performance monitoring only.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information solely to provide the Service — to analyze your resume and return feedback to you. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

Stripe: Handles all payment processing. Subject to Stripe's own privacy policy at stripe.com/privacy.

Anthropic: Powers the AI analysis. Resume content is sent to Anthropic's API for processing. Subject to Anthropic's privacy policy at anthropic.com/privacy.

Render: Hosts the Service infrastructure. Subject to Render's privacy policy at render.com/privacy.

4. Resume Data

Your resume is transmitted securely over HTTPS, processed to generate your audit, and then discarded. We do not store, index, train on, or retain your resume content after your session ends.

5. Cookies and Sessions

We use a session cookie to remember that you have paid and to maintain your access window. This cookie contains no personal information and expires automatically. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

6. Data Security

We use industry-standard security practices including HTTPS encryption for all data in transit. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

You may request deletion of any personal data we hold about you by contacting us at support@clearhire.ai. Since we do not store resume content, the only personal data we may have is what Stripe retains for payment records.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, contact us at support@clearhire.ai.
